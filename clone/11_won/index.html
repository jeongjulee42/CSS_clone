Won Hundred presents:

A NEW TAKE ON LEATHER BOOTS

Together with legendary boot manufacturer Sendra, Won Hundred has designed and produced two signature silhouettes as part of the FW22 collection.

Made in 100% leather, the boots are rooted in our denim universe and combined with our fashion aesthetic, making these boots a perfect fit for every occasion. They are available in two colorways, black and brown, the boots offer a versatile feel and look for everyone.

For FW22, Won Hundred launches 1 pair of womenswear boots and 1 unisex silhouette for both men and women.

Produced in partnership with Sendra

In close partnership with Sendra, we have been working and developing this unique silhouette to accommodate our vision from a design perspective. With more than 100 years in the business, Sendra has played a key part in making this vision come to life.

Today, Sendra serves as an industry leader when it comes to manufacturing and production of leather boots, and through Sendra’s strong sense of quality and creation of long-lasting products – the company has won recognition worldwide.
